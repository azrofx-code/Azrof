About the Department

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering at University of Barishal is dedicated to educating and empowering students in the fields of computing and engineering. Our programs combine theory with practical skills to prepare for real-world challenges.

Vision & Mission

Vision: To become a center of excellence in computing research and education.

Mission: To provide high quality education, promote innovation, and engage in impactful research.
